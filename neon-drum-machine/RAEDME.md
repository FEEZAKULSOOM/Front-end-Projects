A compact and responsive drum machine built with **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**. Play sounds by clicking the pads or pressing keys on your keyboard. Includes two sound banks: **Heater Kit** and **Piano Kit**.

---

## Features

- **Two Sound Banks**: Switch between Heater Kit and Piano Kit.
- **Keyboard & Mouse Support**: Play sounds with keys `Q W E A S D Z X C` or by clicking pads.
- **Volume Control**: Adjust the volume using the slider.
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
- **Neon UI**: Modern neon-style design with hover and active animations.

---

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in a browser.
2. Click on drum pads or press the corresponding keys (`Q W E A S D Z X C`) to play sounds.
3. Use the volume slider to adjust the sound level.
4. Switch between sound banks using the buttons below the pads.
